Egusi Soup

Description

Egusi Soup is a Nigerian classic enjoyed in various forms across the country.

Egusi Soup is a soup made with a white kind of pumpkin seeds, though some people prefer to call it melon seeds.

Ingredients

  • Ground egusi (or pumpkin seeds)
  • Palm oil
  • African crayfish
  • Meats and fish
  • Seasoning
  • Pepper
  • Leafy greens(eg. Pumkin leaf)

Steps

  1. Boil the meats and/or fish you intend to use.
  2. Grind the egusi.
  3. Saute onions in red palm oil, cook egusi in red palm oil then add the boiled meats, crayfish and seasoning
