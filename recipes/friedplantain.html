Fried Platain

Description

Dodo, fried plantain is a delicous dish made from ripe plantain.

Ingredients

  • 4 very ripe plantains
  • vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Fill a pan or skillet about 2 inches deep with oil and heat over medium-high flame for about 5 minutes.
  2. skin and cut plantain into slices about 1-inch thick.
  3. Add plantain slices to hot oil to cook.
  4. Allow the slices to cook for about 5 minutes then flip. The bottoms of each slice should be golden brown, if not, flip back and cook a bit longer. Repeat on the opposite side then remove dodo from oil and place onto paper towel-lined plate.
